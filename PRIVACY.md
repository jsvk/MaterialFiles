# Privacy Policy

Material Files is an Open Source app provided at no cost and intended for use as is.

## Information Collection and Use

This build of the app does **not** collect, use, transmit, or share any personal
information or usage data. There is no analytics, no crash reporting, and no
telemetry of any kind.

Any information you provide (such as remote server credentials or app settings) is
retained only on your device and is never collected or transmitted by the app.

## Third Party Services

This build of the app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or
crash-reporting services (e.g. Google Analytics for Firebase, Firebase Crashlytics).

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on
this page.

## Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us
at https://github.com/jsvk/MaterialFiles.
